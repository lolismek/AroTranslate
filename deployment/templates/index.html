What is AroTranslate?

AroTranslate is the first comprehensive translation system for Aromanian, a unique and endangered Romance language spoken by over 300,000 people in the Balkans. Read more in

About Us.

Usage

You can translate between any two of Aromanian, Romanian and English in any direction. The word limit is around 1000 words, but this depends on the text's constitution. We also recommend using diacritics in the input for the best translation quality.

Note that Aromanian's writing system is not standardized. All translations with Aromanian as target language are made to the Cunia (ã/dz/lj/nj/sh/ts) orthography, but you can adjust for Diaro (ăâ/ḑ/ľ/ń/ș/ț) in top-right corner. If Aromanian is selected as source language, we still recommend the usage of diacritics (Cunia or Diaro, as we convert them to something common). Lastly, translations are generally better when Aromanian is the source language, rather than the target language.

Disclaimer: Aromanian is a very low resource language with many varieties. Due to this, translation quality is experimental, and oftentimes subpar what you might except from a translation system for medium to high resource languages. There may occur translation errors, where certain information is missed or altered. Please use professional human translation services for sensitive issues that require high accuracy.
